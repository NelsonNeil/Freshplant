Floral Plant - $330

Flowering plants, or angiosperms, are one of the most diverse groups of plants. They can range from delicate wildflowers to towering trees, and are found all over the world. The defining characteristic of angiosperms is the presence of flowers, which are the reproductive structures of the plant. These flowers can come in a wide variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, and are responsible for the plant's ability to reproduce via pollination. Flowers are fascinating structures, and studying them can reveal a great deal about the ecology and evolution of the plants that produce them. Additionally, many angiosperms are important sources of food, medicine, and other valuable resources for humans and other organisms.

Dracaena - 500$

Dracaena is a genus of trees and succulent shrubs, with approximately 120 different species. Azalea Plant - 350$

Azaleas are a type of flowering shrub in the genus Rhododendron. They are known for their showy and colorful blooms, which appear in the spring and come in a variety of shades, including pink, purple, red, and white. Azaleas are a popular choice for landscaping and gardening due to their beauty and ease of care. They prefer acidic soil that is well-drained, and are generally hardy in USDA hardiness zones 6-9. Pruning should be done immediately after flowering, and fertilization should be done in the spring. There are many different cultivars of azaleas available, each with its own unique characteristics and requirements. Overall, azalea plants can add a stunning burst of color to any garden or landscape.
